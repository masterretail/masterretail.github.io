WHERE WE COME FROM

Roots in the past, eyes on the future.

Master Retail is a new business, rooted firmly in the excellence of Italian entrepreneurship. Franco and Giampaolo Pisa, the Company’s founders, have been involved directly in the last twenty years, as investors, promoters, and builders, in carrying out important property operations, including the first Shopping Centres in Eastern Europe.

Thanks to this expertise, our team is capable of approaching every operation from all perspectives and with the utmost expertise in every phase of development.
Working in synergy with the biggest national and international companies, today we’re a global partner that supports the Promoter by designing, developing, commercialising and managing the most important of property operations, all brought together by a single objective:
creating value.

WHAT WE DO

Development to create value.

We build on our solid experience in the field, working alongside the promoter to offer the invaluable guidance of a development manager. We coordinate and supervise every aspect of developing every property operation for commercial purposes, directly managing all the strategic functions, right from the decisive moment of drawing up the concept.

The result is a tailor-made service with high value added in all links of the chain. OUR EXPERIENCE

A journey of a hundred years.

The founders of Master Retail can boast of a family tradition of more than a hundred years in the field of entrepreneurship in buildings. The choice to specialise in Retail dates back to the early 1990s, with its own development of Eastern Europe’s first Shopping Centre, in Warsaw, followed by another four, all in Poland, over the course of a decade. Subsequently, the exploration of new markets led to the building of the first Shopping Centre in Moscow and the first in the Balkans, the King Cross Jankomir in Zagreb in 2002.

The return to Italy is connected with the carrying out of major property operations, which was awarded the CNCC Quality Certificate of Merit for best overall quality. But the most innovative project by Franco and Giampaolo Pisa is the Freccia Rossa Shopping Centre in Brescia, the first example of an Urban Centre in Italy, developed in partnership with Sonae Sierra and AIG following the reclamations of an abandoned industrial area.
