WHERE WE COME FROM<\/h1>\n

Roots in the past, eyes on the future.<\/h2>\n
\n

Master Retail is a new business, rooted firmly in the excellence of Italian entrepreneurship. Franco and Giampaolo Pisa, the Company\u2019s founders<\/strong>, have been involved directly in the last twenty years, as investors, promoters, and builders, in carrying out important property operations, including the first Shopping Centres in Eastern Europe.<\/p>\n

Thanks to this expertise, our team is capable of approaching every operation from all perspectives<\/strong> and with the utmost expertise in every phase of development.
\nWorking in synergy with the biggest national and international companies, today we\u2019re a global partner that supports the Promoter by designing, developing, commercialising and managing the most important of property operations, all brought together by a single objective:
\ncreating value.<\/p>\n<\/div>\n

WHAT WE DO<\/h1>\n

Development to create value.<\/h2>\n
\n

We build on our solid experience in the field, working alongside the promoter to offer the invaluable guidance of a development manager<\/strong>. We coordinate and supervise every aspect of developing every property operation for commercial purposes, directly managing all the strategic functions, right from the decisive moment of drawing up the concept<\/strong>.<\/p>\n

The result is a tailor-made service with high value added<\/strong> in all links of the chain. Thanks to our multidisciplinary approach, we are in a position to dialogue with all the professionals involved in the operation, manage them and guide them from the initial briefing to the final output and offer effective solutions for all questions, including those concerning the restoration of abandoned areas and those \u2013 highly contemporary \u2013 linked to the redevelopment and rethinking of existing Shopping Centres<\/strong>.<\/p>\n<\/div>\n
